About Same Power Ministries

Same Power Ministries was founded upon the Holy Spirit leading by Apostle John Enock Eziel in 2014 in Kampala Uganda.

Same Power Ministries, birthed out of the heart of Apostle Paul’s prayer to the Ephesian Christians in; (Ephesians 1:17-22)

..And what is the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to the working of his mighty power, Which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, and set him at his own right hand in the heavenly places.., (Eph.1:19&20).

We are an Apostolic ministry with a mandate to:

  • Usher this generation into the Power and works of the Holy Spirit. This is by presenting the full Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to every soul through evangelistic campaigns; Crusades, Healing and Miracle services, Media and Leadership Conferences with the Holy Spirit manifesting His Power with signs, miracles and wonders, Glorifying God.

Establishing Churches in different parts of the world:

  • To Pastor the un-churched through a local church (Same Power Church) growing Christians into mature saints, disciples who replicate themselves unto to others
  • Revive worship by emerging old hymns and today’s spirit inspired worship.

Right now John Enock Eziel is Head Pator of Same Power Church in Kampala, an arm of of same Power Ministries. He currently also holds "Healing and Miracle Services" and Mission Crusades around Uganda.

OUR CORES

  • Faith: We believe in God the Father, God the Son, and the God the Holy spirit (Trinity)
  • The word : We are guided by the Holy Scriptures of the Bible (2 Tim 3:16)
  • Prayer: We believe that God answers Prayer and we can change the world through Praying all forms of prayers for all People ( 1Tim 2:1-3, I Thess 5:17, Matt 5:13-16).
  • Holiness: We aim to live a consecrated life to God. (Heb 12:14, Romans 12: 1-2, Gal 5:24).
  • Family: We believe in family based Christianity.
  • Dressing: We believe in smart descent dressing in church as a way of honouring God.
  • Unity: We believe in unity with other Churches working together with like minded individuals and organisations (Psalms 133:1-3).
  • Soul winning: The greatest mandate is not optional but it’s mandatory to every Christian and Church (Matt 28: 18-20).
  • Worship: Life is sacred to worship God and we do everything with that mindset (Col 3:23).
  • Love: God is love; we are created in His image (1 John 4:16). We hold all People as brothers and sisters in the Lord (1Corinth 13, Gal 5:22-23).
  • Generosity: All things belong to God and we give generously of all our possessions, finances, services and time unto God in thanksgiving (1Chron 29:6-30.)
  • Excellence: Everything we do, it is to the best humanity can deliver to God with virtue, knowledge and diligence. (1 Pet 1:5-7).
